Indian Ocean is an Indian rock band formed in New Delhi in 1990, who are widely recognized as the pioneers of the fusion rock genre in India.Susmit Sen, Asheem Chakravarty, Rahul Ram and Amit Kilam were band members later Tuheen Chakravorty and Himanshu Joshi bwere officially inducted into the band as successors. After the departure of Susmit Sen in 2013, R ahul Ram is the only founding member who appeared on the band's debut album Indian Ocean. Sanjeev Sharma has collaborated with them as lyricist on many albums.
